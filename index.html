Take this Mental Health Quiz!

Scoring:

Mostly A's: You are in a strong place mentally and emotionally. You prioritize self-care, have good coping mechanisms, and maintain a healthy balance in your life. Keep up the great work!
Mostly B's: You're doing okay, but there may be areas where you could improve. It’s important to take time for relaxation, stress management, and emotional self-care.
Mostly C's: You may be experiencing stress or emotional challenges that could benefit from additional support. Consider reaching out to a professional for guidance and working on building healthier habits.
Mostly D's: You might be feeling overwhelmed or struggling with your mental well-being. It’s important to seek support, whether from a counselor, therapist, or loved ones, and focus on self-care.
This quiz can be a helpful tool for reflecting on your mental wellness. If you find that you're struggling in some areas, it's always okay to seek help and take small steps to improve your well-being. But remember, you are never alone on your journey to seek mental wellness!
